---
title: Quickstart
description: Set up your ContentMaker workspace in under 5 minutes.
---

## 1. Create your account

Sign up at [app.contentmaker.space](https://app.contentmaker.space/signup). No credit card required.

## 2. Add your first client

Navigate to **Clients** and click **New Client**. Fill in the company name and contact details.

## 3. Create a knowledge base article

Go to **Knowledge Base** → **New Article**. Write your content using the built-in editor and assign it to a client.

## 4. Track credits

Each client has a credit balance. Add credits via **Clients** → select client → **Credits** tab.

## 5. Log time

Use the **Timesheets** section to log hours against a client or project.
