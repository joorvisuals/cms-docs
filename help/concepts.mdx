title: Key Concepts
description: The core building blocks of ContentMaker.
---

## Companies & Clients

A **Company** is the top-level entity representing your client. Each company has contacts, a credit balance, and its own knowledge base.

## Knowledge Base Articles

Articles are pieces of content scoped to a company. They support rich text, and can be searched, versioned, and shared with clients.

## Credits

Credits are a flexible unit for tracking prepaid work. You sell credits to clients; consuming a credit logs work against their balance.

## Timesheet Entries

Time entries record hours worked against a company or project. They feed into billing and reporting.

## Users & Roles

Your workspace can have multiple users. Roles control what each user can see and do.
