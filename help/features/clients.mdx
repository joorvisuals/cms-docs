## Overview

Each client is represented as a **Company** in ContentMaker. Companies hold contacts, credits, articles, and time entries.

## Adding a client

1. Click **Clients** in the sidebar
2. Click **New Client**
3. Enter the company name and optional details
4. Save

## Company profile

The company profile shows:
- Contact details
- Current credit balance
- Linked articles
- Recent time entries

## Archiving a client

Archived clients are hidden from the main list but their data is preserved. To archive, open the company → **Settings** → **Archive**.
