## Overview

Timesheet entries record hours worked against a client company. They integrate with credits and can be exported for invoicing.

## Logging time

1. Go to **Timesheets** in the sidebar
2. Click **New Entry**
3. Select the client and optionally a project
4. Enter the date, hours, and description
5. Save

## Viewing entries

Filter entries by client, date range, or user. The total hours and credit impact are shown in the summary row.

## Exporting

Export timesheet data as CSV from **Timesheets** → **Export**. Use this for external invoicing or reporting.
