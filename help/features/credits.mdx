## Overview

Credits are a unit of prepaid work. Clients purchase credits; your team consumes them as work is delivered.

## Adding credits

1. Open a client company
2. Go to the **Credits** tab
3. Click **Add Credits**
4. Enter the amount and an optional note
5. Save

## Credit transactions

Every addition or consumption of credits is recorded as a **transaction**. You can view the full history in the Credits tab.

## Consuming credits

Credits are consumed when you log a timesheet entry linked to a client. The rate of consumption is configurable per project or entry.

<Tip>
  Use credit alerts to notify your team when a client's balance drops below a threshold.
</Tip>
