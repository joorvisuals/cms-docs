title: Billing
description: Manage your ContentMaker subscription and invoices.
---

## Plans

ContentMaker offers monthly and annual plans. View current plan details under **Settings** → **Billing**.

## Upgrading or downgrading

Click **Change Plan** in the Billing section. Changes take effect at the next billing cycle.

## Invoices

Past invoices are available for download under **Settings** → **Billing** → **Invoice History**.

## Cancellation

To cancel, go to **Settings** → **Billing** → **Cancel Subscription**. Your data is retained for 30 days after cancellation.
