---

## Inviting team members

1. Go to **Settings** → **Team**
2. Click **Invite Member**
3. Enter their email address and select a role
4. Click **Send Invite**

They'll receive an email with a link to join your workspace.

## Roles

| Role | Access |
|------|--------|
| Admin | Full access including billing and team management |
| Member | Can manage clients, articles, credits, and timesheets |
| Viewer | Read-only access to all sections |

## Removing a member

Open **Settings** → **Team**, find the member, and click **Remove**. Their past entries are preserved.
