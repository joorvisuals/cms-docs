---
title: Account Settings
description: Configure your ContentMaker workspace.
---

## Profile

Update your name, email, and password under **Settings** → **Profile**.

## Workspace

Set your workspace name and logo under **Settings** → **Workspace**. This branding appears in client-facing views.

## Notifications

Configure email notifications for credit balance alerts, new articles, and timesheet summaries under **Settings** → **Notifications**.
